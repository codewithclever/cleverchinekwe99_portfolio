Project Details

/ ManLand Real Estate Website

ManLand is a clean, modern, and fully responsive Bootstrap HTML template website built specifically for real estate agencies, property listings, and housing startups. Designed with Bootstrap v4.5.0, it is flexible, scalable, and optimized for showcasing residential, commercial, and investment properties with elegance and clarity. Whether you're promoting local listings or high-end properties worldwide, ManLand adapts seamlessly to all devices and browsers.

Features include a hero header with featured property slider, Call-To-Action button, drop-down menu for location, price range, and property type, countdown timer for open house events or limited offers, Instagram Feeds to showcase recent listings, newsletter subscription form for property alerts, and a blog section for real estate insights—and much more. This template comes with a clean, well-structured, and developer-friendly codebase, allowing you to launch your property website right out of the box. Customizing it to match your agency’s identity is effortless. So, start your real estate project with ManLand and elevate your online presence.

New elements added to the package

Visitors love a beautiful and efficient website

Whether you're a designer, developer, or real estate agent with basic web skills, this template is easy to customize and ready to showcase your listings professionally. Among its features, you’ll find a property details lightbox for in-depth views, tabbed content for property features and amenities, video lightbox for virtual tours, a testimonial card slider to build trust with clients, statistics counters to highlight key business figures (e.g., properties sold, years in business), an image slider for partner or developer logos, and dropdown navigation for seamless browsing. Useful extra pages are included—like property articles, blog posts, terms & conditions, and privacy policy—to give your platform a complete and trustworthy feel.

Combined with strategically placed call-to-action buttons, the overall design helps convert visitors into leads by guiding them toward property inquiries, bookings, or agent contact. This template is built with HTML5, CSS3, and Bootstrap 4, ensuring high responsiveness, flexibility, and a smooth experience across all modern devices and browsers.
