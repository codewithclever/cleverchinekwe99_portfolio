/ Hairsal Beauty & Makeup Website

Hairsal is a beautifully designed and fully responsive website created for hair salons, beauty spas, barbershops, wellness centers, and personal stylists. Built with the power of Bootstrap, it combines elegant design with essential features that help salon owners showcase services, book appointments, and engage new clients online.

Why Choose Hairsal?

  • 1
    Elegant & Professional Design

    Hairsal delivers a clean and luxurious look perfect for beauty businesses. From typography to layout, every detail is crafted to reflect trust, relaxation, and professionalism

  • 2
    Perfect for Salons and Wellness Businesses

    Designed with the beauty industry in mind, Hairsal includes sections for services, pricing, team members, image galleries, and more—everything needed to showcase your treatments and team.

  • 3
    Fully Responsive & Mobile-Friendly

    Your website will look amazing on all devices—from smartphones to desktops. Clients can browse services and book appointments wherever they are.

Visitors love a beautiful and efficient website

All designers, developers and tech-savvy people will be able to customize this template with basic web coding skills. Among the features you will find details lightbox for more details information, tabbed content for feature details, video lightbox, card slider for testimonials, statistics numbers, image slider for customer logos, dropdown navigation and useful extra pages for article details, terms.

  • Working Booking / Contact Forms
  • Image & Video Galleries
